Cricket Cricket is an outdoor game played in an oval shape stadium with a wooden bat-and- leather ball between two teams of eleven players each. The main action of the sport happens at the 22-yard pitch which is at the center of the oval shape field. This 22 yard pitch has a set of three wickets at either side. There are three international formats that cricket is played in. These are Test Cricket, One Day Cricket and Twenty 20 Cricket.
